#Ship Rooms

##Summary

##Room Types
###Cockpit
Accommodates 1 to 3 crew members.
Allows control of tier 1 engines and short range communications.

###Bridge
Accommodates 4 to 10 crew members.
Allows control of up to tier 2 engines and short range communications.
